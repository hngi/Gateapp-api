How to get started with GateGuard

Gatepass is a privacy and security measures mobile application, that allows interaction between a resident, and a gateman. The app allows the resident control his visitors by providing expected visitors information to the gateman, who will allow only verified visitors to see the resident. Service providers are also registered, and allowed in, after being confirmed by the resident.

Gatepass is for estates and users who value their security and privacy. The app uses measures like Qr code scan, to verify the identity of visitors.

Gatepass is for all interested estates, as well as individual home owners who see a need for security and privacy measures.

To use Gatepass, register as a resident and invite your gateman. A Gateman can suggest the app to his resident who registers and then invites him. A registered resident invites a gateman on the app, and upon acceptance, the gateman manages the visitors log and verifies visitors before they are allowed in

A registered resident invitea gateman with the gateman's phone number. When the gateman accepts the request, he will be granted access to manage the resident's visitors log. The resident adds a visitor, provides details of the visitor and the visit, and generates the security measures for the gateman to verify said visitor upon arrival.

A gateman registers on the app by accepting an invite from the resident. A confirmed gateman account has access to the resident's visitors log, as well as the details of each visit. The gateman then allows such visitors in, after proper verification.
